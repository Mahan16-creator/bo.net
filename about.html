About Us:

Vivek Choubey Film-Stats is working towards providing you the most accurate stats about the films in India.

We provide the details about:-

~ Latest released movies in India.
~ Yearly release date calendar of movies in India.
~ Domestic (Indian) box office collection of movies which includes:

  • Highest opening day nett collections.
  • Highest opening weekend nett collections.
  • Highest opening week nett collections.
  • Highest lifetime nett collections.
  • Highest lifetime gross collections.
  • Highest week-wise nett collections.
  • Highest circuit-wise nett collections.

~ Overseas (outside India) box office collection of movies which includes:

  • Highest opening weekend gross collections.
  • Highest opening week gross collections.
  • Highest lifetime gross collections.
  • Highest circuit-wise gross collections.

~ Worldwide Box office Collection of movies which includes:

  • Highest opening weekend gross collections.
  • Highest opening week gross collections.
  • Highest lifetime gross collections of movies.

~ Production budget + print and advertisement budget of the movies.
~ No. of domestic footfalls of the movies, which means the no. of patrons watched the movie in cinema halls in India.
~ No. of domestic cinema screens a movie is released on in India.
~ Domestic verdict of the movies in India.
~ Movies that were released today in the past and much more...

We are a no-nonsense website that provides you the authentic and relevant information about films without any gossips and rumours.

This website was founded by Mr. Vivek Choubey in the year 2021 and you can contact us at 'vivek1choubey@gmail.com'.
